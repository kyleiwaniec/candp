      • 03Deaf person's attention 1

      • Take a step-by-step process.
      • Approaching a Deaf person may be enough and your approach may suggest your intention to communicate.
      • You can also wave to get eye contact or tap the person gently on the shoulder.

      04When is it my turn? 2

      • Set up a system in the surgery to that a visual indicator will allow a Deaf person to know when he or she is being called.
      • A monitor in the waiting room is a good idea.
      • If an interpreter is present, make sure you call the patient's name and not the interpreter's name.

      05Scheduling appointments 3

      • Ensure that email or text messages can be used to make an appointment.
      • Why not try an online booking system?
      • It is important that Deaf people manage their health and can book their own appointments.

approach
People, place and purpose inform our work, creating positive outcomes across a broad range of projects.

A grounded philosophy

The core philosophy of our practice guides our way of thinking.

We aim to connect and engage people, striving to improve the places in which we work, live and play. We seek a resolved and balanced response with clear purpose.

This philosophy informs a collaborative approach and creates tangible benefits for all involved.

A collaborative approach

Wallbrink Landscape Architecture (WLA) has developed an open design process to empower people and maximise the potential of each and every project.

We are a team player. We treat every project as unique. We work with our clients and other professionals to harness opportunities and resolve issues and constraints.

We ask lots of questions and think ‘outside of the square’.

Positive outcomes

We harness the potential of outdoor space.

By understanding the needs of people, the opportunities of place, and offering balanced purpose, WLA provides proven creative, sustainable and socially responsible outcomes. Outcomes that add value, engage people and care for the planet.

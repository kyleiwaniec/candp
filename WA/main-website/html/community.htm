Since our company began, Wireless Advocates has continually been a strong partner in our communities.

Our work contributes to improving your local community, as investing in the areas where we live and work is an important part of our culture.

  • You have the opportunity to participate in Wireless Advocates-sponsored volunteer projects
  • Our employees can make valuable contributions in achieving positive community and environmental goals through partnerships with local and national non-profit organizations
  • Every sale helps enrich people’s lives by letting families and friends stay connected or business to prosper

Our employees have focused their volunteer efforts on issues including easing homelessness, early childhood development, hunger, and environmental protection and restoration.  We are driven to continually broaden the scope of our community involvement.

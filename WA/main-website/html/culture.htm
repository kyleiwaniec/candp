Our Company:

Wireless Advocates, a third-party provider of wireless products and services in partners’ retail locations in the U.S. and Puerto Rico, is committed to delivering high-value solutions that enrich people’s lives by helping them connect.  With an expert team of sales professionals, collaborative relationships with leading wireless companies, and a desire to provide superior customer service, we continue to meet and exceed the needs of our customers across the nation.

We operate wireless kiosks and online sales activities for our retail partners Costco, Staples, Army & Air Force Exchange Services (AAFES) and Marine Corps Exchange (MCX) in more than 1,000 locations in the U.S. and Puerto Rico.  Wireless Advocates excels at collaborating with our partners and building a successful, dynamic wireless category within their stores.  Our private label sales kiosks integrate seamlessly into their retail environments, and we strive to deliver a great experience with each customer interaction.

Our Culture:

Most of us at Wireless Advocates have the same “gene”: the one that makes our families and friends always bring us their electronics to figure out, set up, and fix!  Our teammates can’t wait to get new gear and seek the excitement that comes with seeing how it all works.

If this describes you, then Wireless Advocates has an exciting team atmosphere which you’ll love, made up of many different work styles and personalities.  We train you on the details but allow you to be successful at your job, based on your style and abilities.

We give you the power to provide value to customers, and that leads to your increased income, career progression, and achieving success.  Wireless Advocates employees enjoy a fun work environment that guides you and supports your activities.  We do this by:

  • Giving you the ability to personally grow and “commit to win” by being responsible for your own destiny
  • Providing extensive support and coaching to help you perform better and your customers to enjoy your service
  • Creating an atmosphere that allows you to be passionate about the company and enjoy what you do

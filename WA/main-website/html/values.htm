Our Values:

At Wireless Advocates, our teammates of all levels and positions are dedicated to four core values, that help them succeed and our customers to view us as experts:

  • SERVICE: We listen to and exceed our customers’ expectations
  • INTEGRITY: We do the right thing
  • DETERMINATION: We take responsibility and commit to win
  • ENTHUSIASM: We are passionate and enjoy what we do

To help our colleagues be successful, Wireless Advocates offers significant support to help you do your job well. We strive to provide:

  • The retail wireless industry's most thorough sales training program, helping our employees continually perform at the peaks of their abilities
  • Some of the most competitive wireless pricing in the industry, backing your drive to boost your income
  • Extensive company growth, which fosters your options for advancement

At Wireless Advocates, our drive to excel is a passion you'll feel every day!

css3 // 3D Flip Cards

As of June 24th, 2011 the 3D transforms in this demo only work using Safari 5 or the WebKit Nightly build. Chrome supports 3D transforms, but only after you type "about:flags" in the address bar and enable "GPU Accelerated Compositing." Browsers without 3D acceleration just switch the z-index. You won't lose any functionality!

These cards are using -webkit-transform: rotateY() and rotateX(); with some of the 3D settings: -webkit-transform-style: preserve3d; and -webkit-transform-perspective
